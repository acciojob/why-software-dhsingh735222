Why I want to become a developer?

Becoming a software developer has always been my dream. I am fascinated by the endless possibilities technology offers to solve real-world problems. The idea of creating something from scratch and seeing it come to life in the digital world excites me. Furthermore, the constant evolution in the tech industry means that there's always something new to learn, which keeps me motivated and engaged. I want to be part of this innovative field and contribute to shaping the future through software development.
